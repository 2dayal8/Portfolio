Little Blazer Engine CAD Assembly

A fun mechanical CAD class project focused on modeling, assembling, and documenting a small engine-style mechanism.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

A small assembly modeling exercise

This project involved creating a detailed CAD assembly of a Little Blazer-style engine mechanism. The goal was to practice mechanical assembly modeling, component positioning, constraints, and drawing generation in Siemens NX.

While this was a lighter class project, it helped strengthen my familiarity with assembly structure, datum references, drawing views, and communicating mechanical geometry clearly.
